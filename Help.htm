Application Overview

The Roulette for Kovan app lets you be the House that others play Roulette against. The House receives a 5.26% advantage over the player, since payouts do not compensate for the two additional spaces on the board (0 and 00).

There are two versions of the Roulette app:
  • Roulette for Kovan (https://predictionwagers.github.io/Roulette): This version uses Kovan ETH ("play money"), which you cannot exchange for "real money".
  • Roulette for MILLI (https://predictionwagers.github.io/Roulette/milli.htm): This version uses MILLI tokens, which you can exchange for real Ether money.
    You may receive MILLI tokens at: https://TokenUp.app

Token Values: Your Kovan ETH ("play money") is converted to Kovan Tokens, which are each one-thousandth of a Kovan ETH. 1,000 Kovan Tokens are equal to 1 Kovan ETH.
Each MILLI Token is equal to one-thousandth of a real ETH. 1,000 MILLI Tokens are equal to 1 real ETH.

Metamask

This app requires the MetaMask Chrome extension, which allows the Chrome browser to interface with the blockchain, without installing any other software.
Install the Metamask Chrome extension

When you are logged-in to Metamask, select the Kovan Test Network from the dropdown at the top of the Metamask popup screen.

Play Roulette

  1. Select a House from the dropdown box.
  2. Select a Chip Value from the "Each chip =" dropdown box. If the House's Maximum Payout Per Spin is less than 3, then you should select .01 from the "Each chip =" dropdown box, if you want to bet individual numbers (which pay 35-1).
  3. Place bets, by clicking spaces on the roulette table.
  4. The "Maximum Payout (bets placed)" display will show the Maximum Payout that you could receive from the bets that you selected. If a bet causes your Maximum Payout to exceed the House's Maximum Payout Per Spin, an error will be displayed.
  5. After you have selected your bets, click [Spin Wheel], and a Metamask confirmation popup will be displayed.
  6. In less than 30 seconds, a report will show the following information:
    • The Winning Number.
    • A list of the bets you made, and the payout for each bet that you won.
    • The total that you won (or lost) for the Spin.

Create Your House

You can create your own House, and receive the House advantage of 5.26%.
  • House Username: This name will appear in the House dropdown box.
  • Tokens You Will Deposit: This is the total amount that players can bet against you. You can Withdraw your deposit at any time.
  • Maximum Payout Per Spin: This is the maximum Tokens that a player can win in one Spin.

Update Your House

After you create your House, the following information and textboxes will be displayed:
  • Your House Balance: The number of Tokens that your house currently has for players to bet against.
  • Detailed House Report: This link will display a Report showing all the transactions that have affected your House Balance.
  • Your House URL: Give this address to everyone that you know! This address will display the app, with your House selected by default.
  • House Username: You may edit your Username.
  • Maximum Payout Per Spin: You may edit the maximum Tokens that a player can win in one Spin.
  • Tokens to Deposit: You may increase your House Balance, by depositing additional Tokens.
  • Tokens to Withdraw: You may decrease your House Balance, by withdrawing Tokens.
